Project Details:
    Builders app for the game "Elden Ring: Nightreign"
    Will allow users to make chalice builds with relics of their own creation.
    May be one they have in game or a new one they hope to find.
    When builds are made they will reflect the total percentage on a list.
    The builds will also reflect on a living character sheet for their stats and attributes.
